Who We Are

YOUNG BUT EXPERIENCED

We are True Motion Studios — a crew of six high schoolers from St. Vincent and the Grenadines who are dead serious about visual storytelling. Don't let our age fool you. We've built real skills, invested in real equipment, and deliver real results.

From aerial drone footage to ground-level gimbal-stabilized video and sharp photography, we bring technical precision and creative vision to every shoot. Based across Bequia, Kingstown, and Arnos Vale, we serve all of SVG.

We specialize in the fast-paced: cars, sports, and special events — scenes that demand speed, reflexes, and the right gear. That's exactly what we bring.

"We may be in high school, but our footage is anything but amateur. We take every shoot seriously — no exceptions."
